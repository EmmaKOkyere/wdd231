Website Planning and Site Overview

Site Name

Site Name: OksCode Bookstore

This name reflects a modern, tech-savvy bookstore that blends code, creativity, and community. It emphasizes innovation and a digital approach to books and learning.

Optional domain availability: okscodebooks.org

Site Purpose

The primary goal of the site is to provide a platform where visitors can explore our diverse selection of coding, tech, and programming books, register for events, and connect with us digitally. It serves as a resource hub for learners, developers, and tech enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What training workshops or coding events are upcoming?
  • How do I purchase a specific programming book or subscribe to newsletters?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • #4A90E2 — Used for headings and accents.
  • #F4A261 — Used for paragraphs and backgrounds.

Typography

Fonts Selected:

  • Heading Font: "Georgia", serif — for main headings, providing a professional yet approachable feel.
  • Body Font: "Arial", sans-serif — for clarity and readability.
  • Accent/Secondary Font: "Courier New", monospace — for code snippets or technical sections.

Wireframes

Below are basic sketches of the homepage layout:

  • Desktop View: Header with logo, navigation, main content divided into sections (About, Events, Books), footer with contact info.
  • Mobile View: Collapsible menu for navigation, stacked sections for scrolling, a simplified, user-friendly layout.

Welcome to OksCode Bookstore!

Our mission is to promote tech education and provide a modern space for innovation and learning in the digital age.
